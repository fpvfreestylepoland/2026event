About the Event

FPV freestyle is the art of flying first‑person‑view drones with style, flow, and creativity. Now, pilots gather near Katowice to push limits and celebrate the culture of flight.

Expect adrenaline, creativity, and fierce competition across two unforgettable days.

  • International pilots welcome
  • 3.5 inch 4S category
  • 5 inch 6S category

Competition Rules

Judging focuses on the balance of risk, control, and style. Pilots are scored on the criteria below.

  • Style & Flow
  • Difficulty
  • Execution
  • Creativity

Safety: 6S max for 5" class; prop guards in pit areas; spotters required; VTX power per local regulations; fail‑safe checks mandatory.
